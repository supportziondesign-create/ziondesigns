About Zion Designs

Vision meets Reality

Zion Designs is dedicated to transforming ordinary spaces into elegant and functional environments. With a strong focus on quality, creativity, and practical design solutions, we help clients turn their ideas into beautifully designed interiors.

Founded by Samrat and Dibyendu, Zion Designs brings more than 5 years of practical experience in residential and commercial interior design including home interiors, office interiors, modular kitchens, and 3D visualization.

Based in Chandannagar, Hooghly, we proudly serve clients across nearby areas and overall Kolkata with modern design ideas, careful planning, and reliable project execution.
